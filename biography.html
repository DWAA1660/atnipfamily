Meet Our Family

Jim & Marilave

Jim, Dad, is the backbone of the family band, and the rhythm guitar player ever since his children began music in 1996. It was his goal to have an entire "live played" band. He is a busy General Contractor, enjoys hunting, and very much loves his family! He and the family are founders of the Gospel Messengers Music Festivals , which began in Cody, WY in 1998. He is a Gideon in their local camp. His family believes he is a top-notch amazing father and husband.

Ben & Alyssa

Benjamin James,26 the first Banjo picker, married a wonderful girl, Alyssa Richard, on May 1, 2010. He helped his dad in construction since age 13. Then he went into aviation, and is a busy charter/corporate pilot and flight instructor in Cody, WY. He has logged 3000+ hours. He is currently completing his final subjects for a bachelor's degree in Aviation Science. The family sure misses traveling with him, but they are so thankful for the years he could go along on ministry tours! All are so thankful for the Lord's blessings on his and Alyssa's lives!

Kim

Kimberly Dawn,24, is the oldest daughter,and previous fiddle player, and also harmony and lead singer. She is known for her warm smile, and caring ways. She married a very good young man, Kevin Sorrell, on April 18, 2009 and she now lives in Edmond, near Oklahoma City, OK where Kevin works in accounting. She owns and operates a home care business, and keeps busy being an excellent homemaker! They both continue to join the family on some prison ministry singing events and tours. Their first baby, Kandis Danae Sorrell, was born Dec. 29, 2010! Lord willing they plan to bring her on part of the family prison tour this winter!

Joe

Joseph Stan, 22, mandolin and guiter player, was the first born of twins. He’s a quiet, talented and dependable guy! He been dad's right hand man in construction, beginning at age 9. Also he a great hunter and an excellent hunting guide! Joe is an exemplary hard working young man, bringing honor to his Lord! He won 2nd place with his Mandolin in a state contest a number of years ago and was always excellent in school.

Julie
